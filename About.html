About Us

We are proud graduates of ZDSPGC - DIMATALING CAMPUS, Class of 2024-2025. This site celebrates our journey and achievements, marking the hard work, dedication, and unforgettable memories we created along the way. Throughout our academic years, we’ve cultivated skills and forged friendships that will last a lifetime. This yearbook serves as a testament to the support and growth we received from our esteemed instructors and the entire campus community. It’s a reflection of our collective journey through challenges, triumphs, and milestones that led us to this proud moment of graduation.
